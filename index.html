Customer Overview

The Situation

A Fortune 500 workforce solutions enterprise operating across regulated federal and state programs had adopted AI development tools — but end-to-end delivery times remained flat. AI-accelerated code generation was racing ahead of testing, security validation, and deployment processes, creating downstream bottlenecks that eroded any speed gained. The organisation needed a partner who understood how to embed AI safely into regulated delivery pipelines — not just deploy tools and hope for the best.

The organisation was not struggling with innovation capacity. It was struggling with the ability to govern and deploy that innovation safely — in an HR compliance-sensitive environment where a single processing error carries significant legal, regulatory, and reputational consequences.

Industry

Workforce Solutions & HR Technology — processing millions of employment eligibility verifications, HR compliance checks, and regulated workforce benefit claims annually under strict federal and state obligations.

Regulatory Environment

HR compliance obligations govern every workflow touching candidate and employee records. Audit-ready controls, documented approvals, and zero-tolerance accuracy requirements apply at every stage of the delivery lifecycle.

Strategic Need & Challenges

Why the Status Quo Was Unsustainable

Strategic Need

  • Accelerate feature delivery without expanding headcount
  • Automate HR compliance verification and workforce eligibility workflows
  • Maintain compliance, audit-readiness, and security posture required by regulators
  • Eliminate delivery bottlenecks downstream of code generation — testing, scanning, provisioning
  • Prove AI-assisted workflows can scale safely in a regulated enterprise context
  • Create a repeatable, governed delivery model for ongoing AI adoption

Key Challenges

  • Quality assurance overload — test creation lagged far behind AI-generated code volume
  • Security validation delays — manual scanning couldn't match accelerated commit frequency
  • Deployment friction — release gates built for slower cadences became bottlenecks
  • HR compliance exposure — regulatory requirements demand zero-error accuracy in all workforce eligibility and data handling workflows
  • Strict compliance requirements — every change needed audit trails and documented approvals
  • Zero error tolerance — incorrect processing carried regulatory and reputational consequences
